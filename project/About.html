About Us

STARTED IN 2013

Smile Women and Children Foundation is a Nigerian incorporated Not For Profit (NGO) or charity organization with main offices in Abuja. Smile Women and Children Foundation was incorporated on January 10, 2013 with Corporate Affairs Commission number CAC/IT/NO-58881. Among the founding trustees are Awa Majad Al-Karim, Mrs. Zuwaira Doreen Osazuwa Yusuf and Chief Nkem Obiakor.
